About Us

Best Medical Care For Yourself and Your Family

DR Hospital is a multi-speciality hospital with digital X Ray, Ultrasound and Pathology lab facility. The hospital has well equipped OPD with Surgeon, Orthopractic, Gynaecologist & General physician. It has a fully running Dental clinic too. DR Hospital is also accredited by the Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana to meet the needs of its populations.
DR Hospital is proud of its swing bed (extended recovery and rehabilitation) program which allows us to serve many orthopaedic and medical patients requiring longer recovery and rehab services.

Services

Excellent Medical Services

Medicine

Our Medicine Department specializes in providing personalized treatments for various health conditions. Whether it's a minor illness or chronic disease management, we ensure the best healthcare services with advanced medical technology and expert care.

Surgery

The surgery department adheres to recommended national standards of care. Multiple steps are included to achieve . The Hospital surgical department provides quality patient care to achieve the best possible outcome for our patients.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

At DR Hospital, the Department of Gynaecology focuses on the healthcare needs of women, all the way from adolescence to menopause, and later too. We provide a safe and supportive space for women to seek medical care.

Orthopedics & Trauma

Our Orthopedics & Trauma department provides expert care for bone, joint, and musculoskeletal injuries. We offer fracture management, joint replacements, and sports injury rehabilitation, ensuring effective recovery and mobility restoration.

Radiology

Our radiology department provides advanced imaging services such as X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, and ultrasounds for accurate diagnosis. Our expert team ensures high-resolution imaging with minimal radiation exposure, aiding in effective treatment planning.

Physiotherapy

Our physiotherapy department focuses on pain relief, mobility restoration, and rehabilitation. We offer treatments for sports injuries, post-surgical recovery, and chronic pain using manual therapy, electrotherapy, and therapeutic exercises for faster recovery.

X-Ray

Providing quick and precise imaging, our X-Ray services aid in diagnosing fractures, lung infections, and internal abnormalities for timely medical intervention. Our digital X-rays ensure high-resolution imaging for accurate analysis. We focus on low-radiation exposure for patient safety.

Lab Services

We offer state-of-the-art laboratory testing, including blood tests, pathology, and microbiology, ensuring precise and timely diagnostic results for effective treatment. Our labs follow strict quality control for accurate reporting. We also provide home sample collection for patient convenience.

Emergency

Our Emergency department provides 24/7 critical care with rapid response and expert intervention, ensuring quick diagnosis and treatment for urgent cases. Our dedicated team is always ready to handle emergencies efficiently, prioritizing patient safety and well-being.

Dental

Our dental clinic offers comprehensive oral healthcare, ranging from routine check-ups to advanced dental procedures. Our experienced dentists specialize in teeth cleaning, whitening, cavity fillings, root canal treatments, and orthodontic services. We prioritize patient comfort and ensure painless, high-quality dental care using the latest technology.

Ultrasound

We provide high-resolution ultrasound imaging to assist in diagnosing pregnancy-related conditions, abdominal issues, and vascular disorders. Our ultrasound scans are conducted by expert radiologists using advanced equipment to deliver clear and accurate results. Whether it’s a routine pregnancy scan or a specialized Doppler ultrasound..

Operation Theatre

Our modern operation theatres are designed to support a wide range of surgical procedures with the highest level of safety and precision. We specialize in general and laparoscopic surgeries, orthopedic procedures, cardiac surgeries, and emergency trauma operations. With a team of highly skilled surgeons and state-of-the-art equipment.
